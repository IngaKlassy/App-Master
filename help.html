Welcome to AppMaster!

How do I find terms by app?

       There is a list of app icons that can be found by clicking on the AppMaster title at the top of every page. They will take you to the pages which will contain a list of terms with their definitions.

How do I find a term if I forgot the app?

       No problem. Just click the Glossary button at the bottom and you'll find a complete list of terms that we've defined.

What if I can't find the term I'm looking for?

       If you can't find a particular term, you can always let us know by submitting it into the form at the very bottom of the Glossary, and our experts will try to do their best to define it and add it to the database.

I like your app. How do I give 5 stars?

       Ahh, you're too kind. You can rate our app at the bottom of the homepage.
