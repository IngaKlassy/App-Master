Pinterest

Board

is where you'll save related pins. For example, you could create a board for the different recipes you find online.

Pin

is visual bookmark that links from Pinterest to another website.

Pinner

users of Pinterest are commonly called pinners. Whenever ypu find pinners you like, you can choose to follow their pins. This way, whenever they share new pins, you'll see them on your homepage.

Re-pin

means to save. When you save a pin you discover on Pinterest to one of your own boards.

Source Link

is the URL of the website where a pin comes from.
