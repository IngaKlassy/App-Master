Celebrity Syndrome

refers to a situation in which a non-celebrity mistakenly believes he or she is a celebrity. Users with celebrity syndrome will not follow a significant number of Twitter users.

Daily Twitamin

means a tweet that contains useful knowledge that is easy to take in and remember. It is a "daily vitamin" for Twitterers.

Drunktwittering

is the act of posting on Twitter while intoxicated.

Hashtag

is a way of organizing your updates for Twitter search engines. Users prefix a message with a community-driven hashtag to enable others to discover relevant posts. One commonly used hashtag is #followfriday where users network by providing the names of their favourite people to follow on Twitter.

Retweet

abbreviated as RT, is used on Twitter to show you are tweeting something that was posted by another user. The format is RT @username where username is the Twitter name of the person you are retwitting.
